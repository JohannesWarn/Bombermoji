Bombermoji

Play up to 12 people by connecting 4 gamepads (2 persons per gamepad).

Controls:

  • Keyboard 1: Arrows to move, M to bomb.
  • Keyboard 2: WASD to move, T to bomb.
  • Gamepad 1: Left stick or left buttons to move, shoulder button to bomb.
  • Gamepad 2: Right stick or right buttons to move, shoulder button to bomb.
